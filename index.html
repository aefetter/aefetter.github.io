about me

Hi, I’m Anna. I’m a student who loves to communicate. That’s a professional way to say I really like to chat. This trait has made my string of customer service and sales jobs much more pleasant. But it’s also gotten me into trouble, like that time I accidentally applied for the journalism major instead of the minor. I decided to stick it out after talking it out.
My personal interests include anything active and outside including but not limited to running, hiking, skiing, and boating. As for my professional interests, I thrive on discovering what ignites others' passions and diving headfirst into those realms. It's what drew me to the fields of communications and PR; they just seemed like a natural fit. I’m also currently working on upping my technical web design and coding skills to leverage my analytical side.
I’m more of a jack-of-all-trades, master of none. I’m not supposed to admit that. But my energy sets me apart. Those who know me would say I’m a healthy risk-taker. Case in point: that time I impulsively decided to work in Yellowstone National Park while stuck in a during a COVID lockdown freshman year. Keep looking around the site to see where that got me.
